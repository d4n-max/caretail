# Screenshot Notes

All raw screenshots were captured from the running CareTail Android app on `emulator-5554` after building and installing `com.caretail.app`.

Captured raw screenshots:

- `00-onboarding.png` - captured immediately after local onboarding continued; same real app state as home.
- `01-home.png` - real Home/dashboard screen with Luna, quick actions, today's care, upcoming reminders, and latest health note.
- `02-pets.png` - real Pets list screen with Luna.
- `02-pet-profile.png` - real Luna profile screen with profile details, export report, reminders, recent health diary, and documents.
- `03-reminders.png` - real Reminders tab with an overdue Booster vaccine reminder.
- `04-add-reminder.png` - real Add Reminder screen with reminder type, date, time, repeat, and notes fields.
- `05-diary.png` - real Health Diary screen with mood, appetite, energy, and symptoms.
- `05-add-diary.png` - real Daily Check-in form with mood, appetite, energy, symptoms, and notes fields.
- `06-settings.png` - real Settings screen. Captured but not used in final slides because the visible MVP/testing language is not ideal for this awareness post.
- `06-profile-history.png` - real Luna profile/history screen, captured for the "care history together" slide.
- `07-export-report.png` - real Premium screen reached from Export Report. Captured but not used in final slides because the carousel goal is awareness, not premium gating.

Feature/copy adjustments:

- The app supports reminders with types including Medication, Vet Visit, Grooming, Food, Vaccine, and Other. Slide 02 uses "Medications and reminders" instead of only "Medications" to match the real screen.
- The app supports Health Diary entries for mood, appetite, energy, symptoms, and notes. Slides 03 and 04 use profile/diary language rather than inventing a separate vet-note screen.
- Export reports exist but are Premium-gated in the captured app state, so final slides focus on pet profile, reminders, diary, and care records rather than showing the Premium gate.
